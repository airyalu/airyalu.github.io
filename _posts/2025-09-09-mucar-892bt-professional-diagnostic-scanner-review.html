Modern vehicles have moved beyond traditional CAN protocols, and the 892BT keeps pace with support for CANFD (Controller Area Network Flexible Data-Rate) and DOIP (Diagnostics Over Internet Protocol). This means it can communicate with the latest vehicle systems from manufacturers like BMW, Mercedes, Audi, and newer American models that use these advanced protocols. I recently used this feature to diagnose a 2021 BMW that wouldn't communicate with older scanners—the 892BT established connection instantly and identified a gateway module issue that had stumped other mechanics.

ECU Coding Capabilities

This is where the 892BT truly shines for professional technicians. ECU coding allows you to program new control modules, adapt replacement parts, and even activate hidden vehicle features.
