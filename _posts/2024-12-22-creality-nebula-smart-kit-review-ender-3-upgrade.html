Reliability and Failure Prevention

The spaghetti detection feature deserves special attention for its impact on printing reliability. Using computer vision algorithms, the system analyzes the print in progress and can identify when layers have detached from the bed or when extrusion has gone awry—common failure modes that typically result in massive filament tangles. When detected, the system automatically pauses the print and sends notifications to your connected devices, allowing you to address the issue before significant material is wasted. During testing, this feature successfully identified two different failure types: a partially detached print that would have completely failed several hours later, and an extrusion issue caused by a partially clogged nozzle. In both cases, the system caught the problems early enough that resuming after addressing the issues was possible, saving both time and filament. For users printing expensive materials like PETG, nylon, or specialty filaments, this feature alone can pay for the kit in prevented waste.

The interface is clean and responsive, providing all essential information at a glance.
